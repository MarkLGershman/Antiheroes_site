About Anti-Heroes

"I'm ghosting more than a haunted house"

Popon
Anti-Heroes is a Velika based guild that originally came from FF. The guild flourished several years ago and became one of the top guilds on the server for some time. Anti-heroes in the present is a semi casual guild pushes towards improving, but as a secondary to group participation and bonding as a guild to keep a family atmosphere.
We do most content. It generally ranges from lowbi dungeons all the way up to the highest level dungeons. Some of us are ambitious and run dreadspire. We do PvP and bgs. This ranges from CS all the way to Gridiron which opens once in a while. Most of the guild is open to PvP, in addition to running an encouraged CUV every week.
